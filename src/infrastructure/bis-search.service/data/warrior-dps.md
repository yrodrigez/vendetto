1. Hit to 9% (6% if there is a Balance Druid in the raid)
2. Expertise to 6.5% (4.5% with Weapon Mastery)
3. Critical Strike
4. Armor Penetration
5. Strength or Attack Power
6. Agility
7. Haste
8. Hit over 9% (If Dual-wielding)
